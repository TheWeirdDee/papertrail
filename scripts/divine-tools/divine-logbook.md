# Divine Bot Logbook

## Activity

- Record bot runs and observed errors.
- Note any rate-limit messages from the Hiro API.
- Track when cooldown windows expire for `say-gm` and `follow`.

## FAQ

- Why does `all wallets drained or in cooldown` happen?
  - Either wallet balances are too low or all wallets are waiting on cooldown.
- What does `u103` mean?
  - It means the username is already set.
